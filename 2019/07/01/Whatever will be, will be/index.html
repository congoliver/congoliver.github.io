In fact, the real imprisonment of a young person is often not a success, but a self.
It is the back-and-forth confrontation between inner desire and individual ability. It is the final reconciliation of secular values ​​and self-exploration. It is a proposition that you need to spend a lifetime to enlighten.
A teenager who once wanted to squander Fang Fang on Wall Street, and later worked as an accountant in a small county institution, a girl who stayed up late in the serious literary world, and later became a housewife who occasionally gave poems to the laundry for cooking and cooking. Most people This is true for a lifetime. You said that he did not achieve his dreams, but you looked at him and his family, lucky laughter and self-satisfaction. Why do you deny their value?

Our young generation, including myself, always has a very good attitude. Life is not the life you want, you are not the one you want to be. But if you really want to think about it, most of these unhappiness seems to be unnecessary. For example, the expectations of parents, the pressure of relatives and friends, and the comparison with others that are unnecessary.
A few days ago, I saw a teacher from the Iraqi war correspondent, Ante, at a banquet. The words he said gave me a deep impression. The general idea is: If you feel that you have worked hard, it is still very hard, so let Relax yourself, let go of yourself, not your attitude is a problem, but your strategy.

I think so. In this world, there are always people who tell you to work harder, to be faster, or else you will not be able to catch up with others and you will be eliminated. But no one has ever told you, where is the effort to be a head? I’m almost halfway to work hard, can’t I stop doing it? The way I work hard must be the same as everyone else? nobody. Because they don’t know you, don’t know what you want, they won’t let you know about yourself. Over time, you forget who you are and what you want to do.

The Temple of Apollo in the ancient Greek city of Delphi is engraved with seven profound words: Man, know yourself. Socrates is “self-awareness” as his own research. Because of this, it has never been an easy task. This is a process that requires constant refreshing of cognition and trial and error. It requires a lot of reflection, practice, observation and thinking. If you have never known yourself and don’t know what you want, then even if you have achieved the success of the secular definition, you can’t live your own way.
Anything you want to give to a young man who has nothing to do? What I want to say is: Nothing in my opinion is a false proposition.

Don’t listen to the sound of wearing forest leaves, you just take your way, this road may have doubts, pain and deviation, but you don’t panic, this is a necessity of practice. Don’t let the eyes of outsiders become your tie, and don’t let the definition of the world become your burden. Of course, I don’t mean that you have to be independent. You should be able to get along with them. Just you have to know that these should not be the weight of your value. We have all been eager to return home, and we have seen Changan flowers in one day. In the end, we discovered the greatest achievement. It is nothing more than living in the heart.
